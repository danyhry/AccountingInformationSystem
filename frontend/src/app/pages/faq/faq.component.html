FAQ

Find the answers for the most frequently asked questions below

How can I ensure that my expenses and incomes are accurately recorded in the tracker?

Make sure to categorize your expenses and incomes correctly, review and reconcile your records regularly, and enter transactions promptly to avoid errors or omissions.

How can I protect my sensitive financial data in the expenses and incomes tracker?

Make sure to use a strong and unique password for your account, and consider enabling two-factor authentication or other security measures if available. You should also be careful when sharing your data with others or when accessing your account from public or unsecured networks.

How can I keep track of cash transactions in the tracker?

For cash transactions, you can create a category called "Cash" and record each transaction in this category. Alternatively, you could use a mobile app to track your cash expenses and incomes, and then manually enter the data into the tracker.

How often should I review my expenses and incomes tracker?

It's a good idea to review your tracker at least once a month to ensure that your expenses and incomes are accurately recorded and your budget is on track. You may also want to review your tracker more frequently if you're trying to save money or pay off debt, or if you have irregular or variable income.

How can I use the tracker to create a budget?

To create a budget in the tracker, start by categorizing your expenses and incomes and then setting a monthly or yearly budget for each category.

What should I do if I accidentally record a transaction in the wrong category?

Most expenses and incomes trackers allow you to edit or re-categorize transactions after they have been recorded. Simply locate the transaction and change the category as needed.
